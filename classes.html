Our Classes

Over 40 classes each week, taught by certified instructors, for every experience level.

Class Offerings

Each class runs 45 to 60 minutes. All equipment is provided.

Strength

Total Body Strength

A full-body resistance training class using kettlebells, dumbbells, and bodyweight movements. The instructor guides participants through compound exercises that build functional strength and improve posture.

55 min · All Levels

Cardio

HIIT Circuit

Alternating intervals of high-effort work and active recovery, this class uses rowing machines, battle ropes, and plyometric drills to raise cardiovascular endurance and burn calories efficiently.

45 min · Intermediate

Mind & Body

Vinyasa Flow Yoga

A dynamic yoga practice linking breath to movement through a sequence of standing, balancing, and floor postures. Suitable for those with some yoga experience who want to deepen flexibility and body awareness.

60 min · All Levels

Cycling

Rhythm Ride

An indoor cycling class set to music with coached intervals of seated climbs, sprints, and recovery spins. Resistance is adjusted individually, making it accessible to newcomers and challenging for experienced riders.

45 min · All Levels

Low Impact

Barre Sculpt

Drawn from ballet, Pilates, and isometric strength training, this class targets the glutes, thighs, and core through small, controlled movements performed at the barre and on the mat.

50 min · All Levels

Combat

Cardio Kickboxing

A non-contact class combining boxing and martial arts techniques with cardio conditioning. Participants work through combinations of jabs, crosses, hooks, and kicks set to an up-tempo playlist.

50 min · Intermediate
